About Fresh NorthWest Design

For over 20 years, Fresh Northwest Design is the preeminent etched, hand-painted bottle decorating studio.

By utilizing techniques peerless in the industry, coupled with our staff of talented artisans, Fresh Northwest Design provides the highest quality service to the wine industry, charities, corporate market, and the non-profit sector.

The backbone of Fresh Northwest Design is our service-driven commitment and our absolute devotion to researching creative and innovative solutions and creating unrivaled artwork for our clients. We strongly believe that quality is the top priority.

Our goal is to create a product that exceeds your expectations in a timely, professional manner.


About Etching Process

Our clients are looking to add value to their brand, promotion, event, or charitable cause. Our engraved and hand painted bottles are the perfect answer; creating a memorable work of art.

We can work on full or empty bottles of wine. The etching process does not pose a risk to the contents of the bottle in any way.

In addition, we can work on any size bottle, from the standard 750ml bottle, through and including, sizes reaching 27 liters.

While we specialize in wine bottles, we can also engrave and hand-paint any glass or ceramic item (awards, vases, glasses, etc.)
